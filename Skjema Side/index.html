Konklusjon

Undersøkelsen viser at mange fortsatt ikke sorterer avfallet sitt på en bærekraftig måte. Det er behov for bedre opplæring og insentiver for å øke kildesortering blant befolkningen.

Letter to the Prime Minister

Dear Prime Minister Jonas Gahr Støre,

I am writing to urge you to take more action on improving the way we handle food waste and recycling in Norway. Despite significant efforts, there is still a large amount of waste that goes to landfills, which could instead be recycled or composted. Encouraging businesses and citizens to separate their waste more efficiently could lead to significant environmental benefits.

I believe we could also do more to promote recycling technologies and improve the infrastructure available for waste sorting in public spaces. By implementing more user-friendly sorting systems, we can reduce contamination and improve the effectiveness of recycling programs.

Yours Truely, Viggo
